50 Logos in 50 Days in 50 Minutes

Summary

The 50 logos project was an exercise to explore visual elements. Every logo was designed in 50 minutes for 50 consecutive days. This helped me trust in myself as a visual designer. This body of work also showcases my style. With each logo, I tried to explore different techniques of color, texture, dimension, grid system, typography, etc.
