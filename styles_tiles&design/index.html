Styles tile & design

Welcome to Styles, the Wine Country's finest tile and stone showroom!

Since 2001 our showroom has been serving the needs of San Francisco Bay Area clients, builders, architects and designers. We offer a wide selection of unique hand crafted tile, terracotta, mosaics, glass, natural stone and porcelain from around the world with choices ranging from traditional to contemporary and constantly introducing the latest in tile design.

Selecting the right tile or stone color, texture size and shape can be confusing.We are here to assist you in the best possible product selection to meet any budget. By giving special attention to each customer through hand-on design assistance, selecting the right tile or stone has never been more exiting. We are delighted to meet with you in our beautiful showroom to review your architectural plans and help you materialize your dreams.
